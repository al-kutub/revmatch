PropellerAds

Display / PPC · Beginner · link status: join_cta

Net-15/30 common; low minimums; beginner-friendly approval.

Quick start

  1. Create a publisher account on the network’s site (use the Join CTA from your RevMatch shortlist).
  2. Add your site or app and wait for approval if required.
  3. Place the ad tags or script in a non-intrusive slot; start with one format.
  4. Verify impressions in the network dashboard within 24–48 hours.
  5. Tune placements for UX first — aggressive overlays hurt retention more than they help RPM.

Fit notes

  • Traffic fit: under_10k, 10k_100k, 100k_1m
  • Niches: tech, lifestyle, general
  • Geos: global, us, uk_eu
  • RPM band (estimate): $0.5–$3 USD

Estimate — push/onclick mix; geo-sensitive.
